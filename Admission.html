Admission Information

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of previous academic transcripts
  • Birth certificate or national ID
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Medical clearance form

Admission Process

  • Submit online application form
  • Screening and assessment test
  • Interview with school administration
  • Admission confirmation and payment of fees

Fees Structure

  • Application Fee: UGX 50,000
  • Tuition Fee: UGX 2,500,000 per term
  • Boarding Fee (if applicable): UGX 1,000,000 per term
  • Other Charges: Books and uniforms extra
