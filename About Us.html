History

Atiqah Islamic High School was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education rooted in academic excellence and strong moral values.

Mission

To educate and empower students with knowledge, character, and spiritual grounding, preparing them to excel in academics and life.

Vision

To become a leading secondary school that nurtures intellectual, ethical, and socially responsible citizens.

Core Values

Excellence, Discipline, Integrity, Faith, Teamwork, Compassion, and Service to the community.
